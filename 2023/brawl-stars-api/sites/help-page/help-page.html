What's a tag?

• Tag is practically your profile ID

• You can find it on your profile, under your profile picture

Rank viewer

• Shows you the achieved rank on all brawler plus some basic info about a profile.

Brawler trophies

• Shows you how many trophies to push on each brawler to achieve the set brawler trophy milestone.

Desired trophies

• Shows you the least trophies you must push every brawler to to achieve a certain trophy milestone.

Experience bar

• It's a basic in game experience bar, with the level you have achieved.

Trophy viewer

• Shows you the trophies on all brawlers plus some basic info about a profile.

Trophies per minute

• You input: Trophies per win (for example 8), trophies per lose (for example 6) and then your winrate (for example 50(%)). Then it will calculate your theoretical trophies per win, and per minute.

Brawler stats

• Shows you the power level and unlocked items for your brawlers.

Current rank viewer

• Shows you the rank your brawlers are currently on. Doesnt take into account your highest rank.

Star points viewer

• Shows you how many star points you will get, also it will tell you how many you will get for every brawler.

Lost trophies

• Shows you how many trophies you will lose after the season end, also it will tell you how many you will lose on each brawler.
